Student Loan Calculators

Student Loan Payment Calculator

Estimates monthly payments for student loans based on loan amount, interest rate, and term.

Student Loan Refinance Calculator

Helps evaluate potential savings from refinancing student loans by comparing new loan terms with existing ones.

Student Loan Consolidation Calculator

Determines the monthly payment and interest savings from consolidating multiple student loans into a single loan.

Assists in estimating monthly payments under income-driven repayment plans based on income and family size.

Student Loan Prepayment Calculator

Calculates how much time and money borrowers can save by making extra payments or paying off student loans early.

Student Loan Interest Capitalization Calculator

Estimates the impact of capitalizing unpaid interest on student loan balances after periods of deferment or forbearance.

Federal vs. Private Student Loan Calculator

Helps compare the costs and benefits of federal and private student loans, considering interest rates, fees, and repayment options.

Student Loan Payoff Goal Calculator

Assists in setting and tracking goals for paying off student loan debt within a specified timeframe.

Student Loan Interest Deduction Calculator

Estimates the tax savings from deducting student loan interest payments on federal income tax returns.
